# Opportunity Scorecard

Rate each dimension 1-5. Total > 20 = strong opportunity. Total < 15 = pass.

## Scoring

| Dimension | Score (1-5) | Notes |
|-----------|:-----------:|-------|
| **Problem Clarity** | ___ | Is the problem well-defined? Can you explain it in one sentence? |
| **Data Availability** | ___ | Is the data digital, accessible, and sufficient? |
| **Technical Feasibility** | ___ | Can current AI technology solve this reliably? |
| **Budget / Willingness to Pay** | ___ | Do they have budget? Have they indicated willingness? |
| **Time to Value** | ___ | Can you show results in 2-4 weeks? |
| **Strategic Fit** | ___ | Does this match your skills and target market? |

**Total: ___ / 30**

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | No / not at all |
| 2 | Weak / unlikely |
| 3 | Moderate / possible |
| 4 | Strong / likely |
| 5 | Excellent / certain |

## Decision

- **25-30**: Pursue aggressively. Prepare proposal within 1 week.
- **20-24**: Pursue. Schedule follow-up, explore further.
- **15-19**: Maybe. Need more information before committing.
- **Below 15**: Pass. Politely decline or refer to someone else.
