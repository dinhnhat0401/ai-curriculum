# Discovery Call Template

## Pre-Call Research
- [ ] Company website reviewed
- [ ] Industry and size identified
- [ ] LinkedIn profiles of attendees reviewed
- [ ] Current tech stack guessed (job postings, case studies)
- [ ] 3 hypotheses prepared about their pain points

## During the Call

### Company Context
- Company name:
- Industry:
- Size (employees):
- Current tech stack:
- Who I spoke with (name, role):

### Current Process
- What process are we discussing?
- How is it done today? (step by step)
- Who does it? How many people?
- How many hours/week does it take?
- What tools do they use?

### Pain Points
- What's the biggest frustration?
- What goes wrong? How often?
- What does a mistake cost? (time, money, reputation)
- Their exact words (quotes):

### Previous Attempts
- Have they tried to solve this before?
- What happened?
- Why didn't it work?

### AI Readiness
- Do they have the data needed?
- Is the data digital and accessible?
- Who would need to approve a solution?
- What's their timeline?
- What's their budget range?

## Post-Call Scoring

Use the opportunity scorecard to rate this opportunity.

## Follow-up
- [ ] Send thank-you email within 24 hours
- [ ] Share one relevant insight or resource
- [ ] Schedule follow-up if opportunity scored > 20/30
