# Sales Materials: Packaging Your Work

**Time: ~6 hours | Templates: `case_study_template.md`, `proposal_template.md`**

---

## The Sales Toolkit

| Material | Purpose | When to Use |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **Demo video** (5 min) | Show the product working | Cold outreach, LinkedIn, email follow-up |
| **Case study** (1 page) | Prove you've delivered results | Proposals, website, meetings |
| **Presentation** (30 min) | Presale meeting with stakeholders | Scheduled meetings |
| **Proposal** (3-5 pages) | Formal project scope and pricing | After discovery, before signing |

## Pricing AI Consulting

| Engagement Type | Typical Price | Timeline |
|----------------|--------------|----------|
| Discovery / Assessment | $2,000-5,000 | 1-2 weeks |
| Pilot / POC | $10,000-25,000 | 4-6 weeks |
| Production Build | $25,000-100,000+ | 2-4 months |
| Monthly Retainer | $5,000-15,000/month | Ongoing |

**Never price by the hour.** You'll be penalized for being efficient. Price by the value you deliver.

**Value-based pricing:** If you save a company $500K/year, charging $50K is an easy yes.

## Writing a Case Study

Structure:
1. **Title**: [Result] for [Company Type] using [Technology]
2. **The Challenge**: What was the problem? How bad was it?
3. **Our Approach**: What did you build? What technology?
4. **Results**: Specific numbers. Hours saved. Accuracy achieved. Cost reduced.
5. **Tech Stack**: Table of tools used.

Lead with the business outcome, not the technology. Executives don't care about embeddings -- they care about hours saved.
